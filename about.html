Hi there! Welcome to my Blog (:

My name is Kátia Nakamura and I'm from Brazil - born and raised.

I received a master's degree in Computer Science (Operational Research) at Federal University of São Paulo. During my bachelor's degree - also in CS - I got an ERASMUS MUNDUS Scholarship to study abroad for one year at the Masaryk University in Brno, Czech Republic (from 2010 and 2011).

During my master's degree I had an opportunity to be Research Assistant at Laval University in Quebec City, Canada for 3 months. As a result of the research my article "The Traveling Backpacker Problem: A computational comparison of two formulations" was published by the Journal of the Operational Research Society.

Currently I'm based in Brno, Czech Republic working as Tech Team Lead at Kiwi.com.
